Master Moroccan Darija with over 1,000 words and phrases designed for Spanish speakers. Whether you are traveling to Morocco, connecting with Moroccan friends and family, or simply love languages — this app gives you the vocabulary people actually use in the street, not textbook Modern Standard Arabic alone.

In Morocco, Darija is the everyday spoken language. This app helps you learn it systematically with Arabic script, Latin transliteration, and Spanish meanings — plus cultural notes so you sound natural and respectful.

  • Smart study — Flashcards with flip animation, spaced repetition (SRS), interactive quizzes, favorites, and powerful search across Darija, transliteration, and Spanish.
